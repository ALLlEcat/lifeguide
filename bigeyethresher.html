Bigeye Thresher Shark

Lifespan: 20 years

Top speed: Bigeye thresher sharks usually swim at around 2 mph

Weight: 350 lbs

Diet: Benthic and pelagic fish, small squid and crustaceans

Predators: Orcas and larger sharks

Range: Temperate and tropical oceans worldwide

Conservation Status: Vulnerable

Fun Fact: Bigeye thresher sharks are distinguishable from other thresher shark species by their large, upward-facing eyes.


Fun Fact: Bigeye threshers exhibit diel vertical migration, which means it stays deeper in the water during the day and comes up to the surface at night to feed.
