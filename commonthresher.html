Common Thresher Shark

Lifespan: 20-50 years

Top speed: About 30 mph

Weight: 500-700 lbs

Diet: Small fish, including mackerel, herring, and small squid

Predators: Orcas and larger sharks

Range: Tropical and temperate oceans worldwide

Conservation Status: Vulnerable

Fun Fact: Thresher sharks' tails can be up to half the length of their bodies.


Fun Fact: Common thresher sharks have a heat exchange system in their body that allows them to maintain a body temperature higher than the water they are in, making them endotherms.
