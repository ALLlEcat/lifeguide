Indian Flying Fox

Lifespan: 15-30 years

Top speed: 19 mph, or 13 mph over long distances

Weight: 3.5 lbs

Diet: Mainly fruits, such as mangoes and bananas, pollen, nectar, flowers, occasionally insects

Predators: Raptors, including the crested serpent eagle, black kites, and spotted owlets, rat snakes, and mammals, including mongooses and palm civets

Range: Widespread across Indian subcontinent

Conservation Status: Least concern

Fun Fact: With a wingspan of up to 5 feet, Indian flying foxes are the largest bat in the world.


Fun Fact: Around 20% of all mammal species are a species of bat.
